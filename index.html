LIGHTSABER BUILDER

Long long ago in a galaxy far far away...

Welcome to the Lightsaber Customizer, a place where you can create your own unique lightsaber. This website offers a variety of features to help you design the perfect lightsaber:

1. Lightsaber Builder: Choose from a selection of hilts, blades, and addons to customize your lightsaber. Use the color picker to select the blade and overall colors. Preview your creation in real-time and make adjustments as needed.

2. Gallery: Share your lightsaber creations with others and view lightsabers designed by other users. Upload your lightsaber images to the gallery and leave messages for other users.

3. Storage: Save your lightsaber designs to your private storage. Access your saved designs anytime, zoom in for a closer look, download, or edit them.

4. Help: If you need assistance, click the help button to get in touch with our support team.

We hope you enjoy using the Lightsaber Customizer. May the Force be with you!
